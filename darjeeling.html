Darjeeling

Tea Factory of India

Darjeeling is one of the most magnificent hill resorts in India. It pleases with its scenic panorama and captivating aroma of tea. If you’re in search of an offbeat place somewhere in the peace of northeast, then you can’t go wrong with Darjeeling.

How to reach there?

Darjeeling is a town in India's West Bengal state, in the Himalayan foothills. The nearest airport from Darjeeling is Bagdogra ,approximately 65kms away and the closest railway station is New Jalpaiguri.

Top things to do?

Thanks to the position of Darjeeling in Himalayan foothills, there are a number of excellent trekking trails you can find around the city.A visit to the city of Darjeeling is incomplete without a joyride at the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway toy train. You can spend an evening in the bustling Batasia Loop, one of major tourist attractions of Darjeeling.Ghoom Monastery is another attraction you can visit.

Moving at a snail pace over the hilly train, no one wants to miss the experience of Darjeeling Himalayan Railways. This 88-km long railway track is best known for its toy train which gushes amid the misty hill side and offers absolutely marvellous views of the valley.From the exquisite landscapes, verdant tea estates, lush green forests, breath-taking flora and fauna, one can witness the beauty of Darjeeling in all its glory.

Ghoom Monastery, also known as Yiga Choeling, is amongst the top tourist attractions in Darjeeling due to its massive collection of rare Buddhist manuscripts and records. Another attractive thing about the Monastery is its walls that are painted with depictions of Buddhism wisdom and Tibetan art. 15-feet high statue of Maitreya Buddha in the monastery invites flux of tourists all through the year.

If you love being outdoors and surround yourself with the freshness and calm of nature, Tiger Hill is the most ideal place for you to visit when you are in Darjeeling.It is the most famous mountain peak in Darjeeling best known for its trekking routes and panoramic views of Mt. Everest and Kanchenzonga.It is a majestically rich experience.
