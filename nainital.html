Nainital

Lake city of India

Nainital is a glittering jewel in the Himalayan necklace, blessed with scenic natural spledour. Being a popular destination among tourists, there are many places of interest in and around Nainital that can be visited with family and friends.

How to reach there?

Nainital is located in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand.Kathgodam railway station, about 23 kms away from Nainital, is the nearest railhead serving this gorgeous hill station.Among airways,the Pantnagar airport is the nearest airport to Nainital which is situated at a distance of 70 kms.

Top things to do?

If we check out the most visited tourist spots in Nainital, Snow view point is surely the most visited and loved spot by people all over. And if you want to have something out of box , go for Mall road. Needless to say, you can visit numbers of lake (Nainital lake, Bhimtal lake, Sattal and many more).

Know More

Naina peak is highest hill top and one of the famous tourist spots in Nainital.It offers breathtaking bird's eye view of the Nainital Town, the Himalayas and furthermore , the immense breadths of the Kumaon region.It is a delight for individuals who adore strolling. One can go for long strolls, breath fresh air and appreciate the picturesque excellence.If you are one of those people who prefer to go the extra mile to have the best results, Naina Peak is the ideal place for you.

Located at an altitude of 2118 m, with the incredible view of valleys, snow clad mountains and lakes, Land end is a must visit on a tour to Nainital.

Your tour to Nainital is incomplete if you miss the chance of visiting Sattal Lake. As the name says it is a body of 7 freshwater lakes in the lower Himalayan range. It is an unforgettable spot having cluster of lakes placed amidst the oak forest,comparable with Westmoreland of England.If you are an avid birdwatcher, this place shall not disappoint you, you will find a lot of migrated birds in the vicinity.
